What is BeReal?

- a french App released 2020

- an app with which you can share daily random moments as photos

- you get a random notification every day to post a photo within 2 Minutes / if you manage to post it ontime you get 2 more photos to share with your friends

- BeReal is about sharing private moments with your friends and not about connecting with new ones

Features:you can post pictures, react to them, comment on them, add new friends and create a new profile

Target:young People, that are on her Phone 24/7

Accessed data:location, music streaming connections, camera, name, screenshots

UI/UX:minimalistic, clear - 1 picture on screen/ vertical scrolling => it's possible to interact and share
